Welcome to Ceyloan Travellers

About Us

Ceyloan Travellers is a trusted, family-run Sri Lanka travel company dedicated to creating authentic and memorable travel experiences across the island. As a local Sri Lanka tour operator, we specialize in carefully designed itineraries that showcase the country's stunning beaches, lush hill country, wildlife safaris, ancient heritage sites, and vibrant culture.

Our deep local knowledge allows us to offer custom tours in Sri Lanka that suit couples, families, solo travelers, and small groups. We believe travel is more than sightseeing—it is about meaningful connections with people, places, and traditions.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading Sri Lanka tour operator recognized globally for sustainable, authentic, and responsible travel experiences. We envision a tourism industry where travel positively impacts local communities, protects the environment, and preserves Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, responsible travel services in Sri Lanka while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit both travelers and local communities. We are committed to supporting local employment, partnering with community-based businesses, and encouraging cultural exchange through authentic travel experiences.
