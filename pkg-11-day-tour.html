Private Itinerary - 01

Arrival Sigiriya Polonnaruwa Anuradhapura Kandy Nuwara Eliya Depature

The Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the town’s most famous attraction. This ancient complex consists of five caves adorned with over 150 Buddha statues and beautifully preserved wall paintings. Set on a rocky hill, the temple also offers panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Visitors are advised to dress modestly and be prepared for a short climb to reach the caves.
