Explore Around Sri Lanka : 14 Day Tour Package

Arrival Anuradhapura Jaffna Trincomalee Batticaloa Polonnaruwa Sigiriya Kandy Nuwara Eliya Ella Polonnaruwa Yala National Park Galle Depature

Explore Sri Lanka in 14 unforgettable days—ancient cities, lush tea hills, wildlife safaris, golden beaches, rich culture, scenic trains, and warm hospitality across this tropical island paradise.

Day One

• Transfer to Anuradhapura: Approximately 169 km / 3 hours 40 minutes drive.

• Check into Hotel: The Lake Front Hotel Anuradhapura.

• Sightseeing in Anuradhapura:

• Jethawanaramaya Stupa and Museum

• Twin Ponds

• Samadhi Buddha Statue

• Abayagiriya Stupa

• Elephant Pond and Moonstone

• Thuparamaya

• Ruwanweliseya Stupa

• Sri Maha Bodhiya (Sacred Bodhi Tree)

• Other Monuments


Overnight: The Lake Front Hotel Anuradhapura

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Two

• After Breakfast: Depart for Jaffna. Approximately 196 km / 3 hours 50 minutes drive.

• Optional Stop: Mihinthalaya, the cradle of Sri Lanka’s Buddhism.

• Check into Hotel

• Leisure: Explore Jaffna at your own pace.


Overnight: The Fox Resort Jaffna

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Three

• After Breakfast: Explore Jaffna.

• Nallur Temple

• Keerimalai

• Jaffna Library

• Delf Island

• Kandarodai

• Light House

• Lake Yamuna

• Jaffna Clock Tower


Overnight: at Jaffna

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Four

• After Breakfast: Depart for Trincomalee. Approximately 240 km / 4 hours 27 minutes drive.

• Check into Hotel

• Leisure: Rest of the day at the hotel and beach.


Overnight: at Trincomalee

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Five

• Visit: Pigeon Island National Marine Park.

• Snorkeling

• Scuba Diving.

• Whale and Dolphin Watching

• Leisure: Rest of the day at the hotel.


Overnight: at Trincomalee

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Six

• After Breakfast: Depart for Passikudha. Approximately 124 km / 2 hours 42 minutes drive

• Thirukoneshwaram Temple

• Fort Fedrick

• Marble Beach

• Kinnya Bridge


Overnight: at Passikudha

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Seven

• Explore: Batticaloa :

• Batticaloa Fort

• Batticaloa Gate

• Kalladi Bridge

• Light House

• St. Mary’s Cathedral

• Gandhi Park

• Boat Ride in Batticaloa Lagoon

• Leisure: Return and relax at the hotel.


Overnight: at Passikudha

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Eight

• After Breakfast: Depart for Sigiriya. Approximately 125 km / 2 hours 45 minutes drive

• Optional Stop: Visit Polonnaruwa Ancient City.

• Stop: Parakrama Samudraya (Lake Reservoir)

• Check into Hotel: at Sigiriya.

• Activity: Climb Pidurangala Rock for sunset.


Overnight: at Sigiriya

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Nine

• After Breakfast: Depart for Kandy. Approximately 99 km / 2 hours 39 minutes drive

• Optional Stop: Dambulla Cave Temple

• Nalanda Gedige (Center Point of Sri Lanka)

• Check into Hotel: at Kandy

• Activity: Visit Sacred Tooth Relic Temple.

• Walk around Kandy Lake.

• Kandy Cultural Dance Show.


Overnight: at Kandy

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Ten

• After Breakfast: Depart for Ella. Approximately 138 km / 3 hours 52 minutes drive

• Stops: Nuwara Eliya Post Office

• Grand Hotel for pictures

• Train Ride: Hop on the train from Nanuoya to Ella or Haputale Railway Station.

• Leisure: Relax at the hotel.


Overnight: at Ella

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Eleven

• After Breakfast: Explore Ella

• Activities: Hike Little Adams Peak

• Nine Arch Bridge

• Lipton Seat

• Visit Dombetenne Tea Factory

• Leisure: Enjoy the surroundings of Ella.


Overnight: at Ella

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Twelve

• After Breakfast: Depart for Yala. Approximately 90 km / 2 hours 12 minutes drive

• Stops : Ravana Waterfalls

• Buduruwagala Ancient Buddha Monument

• Check into Hotel

• Activity: Afternoon Safari at Yala National Park.


Overnight: at Yala

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Thirteen

• After Breakfast: Depart for Galle. Approximately 162 km / 2 hours 55 minutes drive.

• Stops : Mirissa Beach

• Coconut Hill Mirissa

• Turtle Hatchery

• Stilt Fishermen.

• Evening: Walk around Galle Fort.


Overnight: at Galle

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Fourteen

• After Breakfast: Depart for the airport, allowing 4 hours before your flight departs.


Tour End
