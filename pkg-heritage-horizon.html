About This Tour

Embark on an unforgettable 5-day journey that combines the spiritual essence of Sri Lanka's ancient kingdoms with the tropical beauty of its northeastern coastline. This tour offers a perfect balance between cultural immersion and beach relaxation.

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Anuradhapura, one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and the cradle of Sri Lankan Buddhism. Then, unwind in Trincomalee, home to some of Sri Lanka's most beautiful beaches, pristine coral reefs, and rich maritime history.
