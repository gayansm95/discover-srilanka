About This Tour

Embark on a captivating 3-day journey to Jaffna, the cultural heartland of Sri Lanka's Tamil community. This carefully curated tour offers an authentic experience of Northern Sri Lanka's rich heritage, from ancient Hindu temples to colonial-era architecture and vibrant local markets.

You'll explore the iconic Nallur Kandaswamy Temple, witness the serene beauty of the Jaffna Peninsula's coastal landscapes, taste authentic Jaffna cuisine, and interact with local communities to gain deeper insights into Tamil culture and traditions.
