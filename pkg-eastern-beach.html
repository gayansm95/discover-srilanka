About This Beach Paradise Tour

Embark on the ultimate 7-day beach holiday exploring Sri Lanka's spectacular eastern coastline. This tour combines relaxation, adventure, and cultural experiences across four of Sri Lanka's most famous beach destinations.

From whale watching in Trincomalee and lagoon tours in Batticaloa to the shallow waters of Pasikuda and world-class surfing in Arugam Bay, this tour offers the complete Sri Lankan beach experience. Enjoy pristine beaches, water sports, fresh seafood, and authentic coastal culture.
