Experience the best of Sri Lanka's cultural triangle, hill country, and scenic beauty – from Sigiriya rock to Ella's iconic nine arches. Half‑board stays and seamless transfers.

Sigiriya · Kandy · Nuwara Eliya · Ella

UNESCO & Hill Country Wonders
Flexible scheduling: Times and visiting places can be negotiated based on your requirements and flight schedule. This itinerary is fully customizable.

Sigiriya Lion Rock – iconic UNESCO fortress

This 5‑day combo covers the cultural triangle and the lush hill country. Begin with the majestic Sigiriya rock and Dambulla cave temple, then proceed to Kandy for the Temple of the Tooth and a cultural dance show. Continue to Nuwara Eliya (Little England) and Ella, where you'll see the famous Nine Arches Bridge and Rawana Falls. End with a scenic drive back to the airport. Carefully planned half‑board accommodation and realistic travel times ensure a comfortable, immersive experience.
