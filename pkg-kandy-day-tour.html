Eight Hours Around Kandy – Day Tour

UNESCO World Heritage City

Sri Dalada Maligawa - Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

Kandy, a UNESCO World Heritage City, is the cultural heart of Sri Lanka and the capital of the Central Province. It is one of the island's most beloved destinations, welcoming both local and international visitors. At the center of its charm stands the Sri Dalada Maligawa, the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic of Lord Buddha. Renowned for its magnificent architecture, ornate decorative walls, golden roof, intricate woodwork, and fascinating museums, the temple offers a deeply spiritual experience. Daily poojas (religious services) held in the morning, midday, and evening add to its serene and timeless atmosphere.
