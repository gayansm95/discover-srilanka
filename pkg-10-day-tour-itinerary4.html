Negombo's shores, Sigiriya's rock, Kandy's culture, Nuwara Eliya's mist, Ella's peaks, Yala's wildlife, Galle's fort, and Colombo's skyline – a 10‑day journey through heritage, hills, and horizons.

Negombo · Sigiriya · Kandy · Nuwara Eliya · Ella · Yala · Galle · Colombo

10 Days · Culture, Hills & Wildlife
Flexible scheduling: Times and visiting places can be negotiated based on your requirements and flight schedule. This itinerary is fully customizable.

This 10‑day tour takes you on a grand adventure through Sri Lanka's most iconic landscapes. Begin with a relaxing evening at Negombo beach, then explore the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya and cave temples of Dambulla. Experience Kandy's sacred culture, journey through the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya, discover Ella's scenic beauty, enjoy a thrilling safari in Yala National Park, explore Galle's colonial charm, and finally visit Colombo's Gangaramaya Temple before departure. Half‑board accommodation and seamless transfers throughout.
