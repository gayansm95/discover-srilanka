Sri Maha Bodhi, Anuradhapura – oldest documented tree in the world

This 5‑day journey combines the cultural heart of Sri Lanka with the serene east coast. Begin in the first ancient capital Anuradhapura, then head to the harbour city Trincomalee for beach relaxation and Hindu shrines. Return to the cultural triangle to conquer Sigiriya Rock and the Dambulla cave temple, before ending in the hill capital Kandy. Carefully planned half‑board accommodation and realistic travel times ensure a comfortable, immersive experience.
