Ancient Kingdoms Experience

Sri Lanka's Cultural Triangle - Ancient Kingdoms

Journey through Sri Lanka's Cultural Triangle, the heartland of ancient Sinhalese civilization. This 4-day tour takes you through the island's most significant archaeological sites, sacred temples, and cultural treasures. From the ancient capital of Anuradhapura to the majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress and the sacred Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, experience the best of Sri Lanka's rich heritage and ancient architecture.
