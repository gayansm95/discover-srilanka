4 Days 3 Nights Tour from Colombo

Day One

• Transfer to Anuradhapura & Check into Hotel: The Lake Front Hotel Anuradhapura.

• Sightseeing in Anuradhapura :

Jethawanaramaya Stupa and Museum

Twin Ponds

Samadhi Buddha Statue

Abayagiriya Stupa

Elephant Pond and Moonstone

Thuparamaya

Ruwanweliseya Stupa

Sri Maha Bodhiya (Sacred Bodhi Tree)


Overnight: The Lake Front Hotel Anuradhapura

Meal Plan: Half Board (One DBL room and One TPL room)

Day Two

8:30 AM: Depart for Kandy after breakfast..

9:15 AM: Visit the Dambulla Golden Temple.

10:15 AM: Make a stop at Nalanda Gedige, considered the geographical center of Sri Lanka.

11:30 AM: Pay your respects at the Sri Muthumari Amman Hindu Temple in Matale.

2:45 PM: Embark on a spiritual journey to the Temple of the Tooth.

5:00 PM: Immerse yourself in the vibrant colors and rhythms of a Kandyan Cultural Dance Show.
