Essential Sri Lanka Travel Tips

Make the most of your Sri Lankan adventure with our insider advice for international travelers

Visa & Entry Requirements

Most visitors need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA). Apply online before arrival for smooth entry. Keep passport valid for 6+ months.

Best Time to Visit

December to April for west/south coasts, April to September for east coast. Year-round good weather somewhere on the island!

Food & Dining Tips

Try rice and curry, hoppers, and fresh seafood. Bottled water recommended. Ask for "no spicy" if you prefer milder food.

Currency & Payments

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR) is local currency. Credit cards accepted in cities, but carry cash for remote areas and small vendors.

Transportation

Scenic train rides through tea country are a must. Tuk-tuks for short trips, private drivers for longer journeys recommended.

Cultural Etiquette

Dress modestly at temples, remove shoes before entering. Use right hand for eating and giving/receiving items.
