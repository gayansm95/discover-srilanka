Itinerary 2 – Golden Heritage & Coast

  1. Home

Negombo's shores, Anuradhapura's sacred city, Trincomalee's temples, Sigiriya's rock, Kandy's culture, Bentota's beach, and Colombo's Gangaramaya – a perfect blend of heritage and coast.

Negombo · Anuradhapura · Trincomalee · Sigiriya · Kandy · Bentota · Colombo

7 Days · Heritage & Eastern Coast
Flexible scheduling: Times and visiting places can be negotiated based on your requirements and flight schedule. This itinerary is fully customizable.

This 7‑day tour begins with a relaxing evening at Negombo beach, then journeys to the ancient capital Anuradhapura. Next, explore the east coast with Trincomalee's temples and Nilaveli beach, return to the cultural triangle to climb Sigiriya rock and visit Dambulla caves, experience Kandy's sacred tooth relic and dance, unwind on Bentota's golden shores, and finally discover Colombo's Gangaramaya Temple before departure. Half‑board accommodation and comfortable transfers throughout.
