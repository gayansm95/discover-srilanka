Welcome to Ayubowan Tours

About Us

Ayubowan Tours is a trusted, family-run Sri Lanka tour operator creating authentic, personalized journeys across the island. From golden beaches and lush hill country to wildlife safaris and ancient heritage sites, we design unforgettable experiences rooted in local culture, sustainability, and genuine Sri Lankan hospitality.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized Sri Lanka tour operator, delivering authentic and sustainable travel experiences. We aim to protect nature, celebrate culture, empower local communities, and inspire responsible travel while creating unforgettable journeys across Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, responsible travel experiences in Sri Lanka through ethical, eco-friendly tours that support local communities, protect nature, and celebrate culture—while providing personalized service and unforgettable journeys for every traveler.

Design Your Sri Lanka Journey

Every traveler is unique. We design personalized Sri Lanka journeys that match your style, interests, and dreams.

14 Day Tour Package

A perfectly paced 14-day Sri Lanka tour combining timeless culture, breathtaking landscapes, wildlife encounters, and coastal relaxation.

Kandy Day Tour

Discover the cultural charm of Kandy in one day, visiting sacred temples, scenic lakes, royal heritage sites, and vibrant local markets.

Sri Lanka's "Cultural Triangle" Tour

Step into Sri Lanka’s Cultural Triangle, a timeless region linking the ancient kingdoms of Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, and Kandy, enriched by UNESCO World Heritage sites, sacred Buddhist monuments, the legendary Sigiriya Rock Fortress, and the mystical Dambulla Cave Temples.

Sri Lanka Highlights - 11 Day Tour

Explore Sri Lanka’s most iconic destinations in 11 days, from sun-kissed beaches and ancient wonders to misty mountains and wild safaris.

Dambulla , Sigiriya & Kandy Tour

Journey through Sri Lanka’s Cultural Triangle, discovering Dambulla’s cave temples, the majestic Sigiriya Rock, and the historic city of Kandy.

Discover Northern Sri Lanka

Discover North Sri Lanka’s cultural heritage, stunning beaches, and historic sites—an essential journey for every traveler seeking authentic experiences.

Heritage & Horizon Tour

Experience the perfect blend of rich cultural heritage and breathtaking beaches, offering an unforgettable, premium journey through east Sri Lanka.
